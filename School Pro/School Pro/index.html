The Government School, Rajanakunte

The Government Primary School in Rajanakunte, Karnataka, traces its roots back to 1945. Nestled on a 3-acre expanse, it caters to students from Std 1 to 8. The medium of instruction for Std 1-5 is Kannada, while for 6th-8th, it extends to both Kannada and English.

Established in 2009, the Government High School in Rajanakunte, Bangalore, Karnataka, sprawls across 4.5 acres. This institution provides education for 9th and 10th grades in both Kannada and English mediums.

Shri S R Vishwanath

“MLA, Yelahanka | Member, TTD Board | Former Chairman, BDA”

Singanayakanahalli Ramaiah Vishwanath is an Indian Politician who is the current MLA from Yelahanka constituency since May 25, 2008. He is the former Chairman of Bangalore Development Authority and presently a Member of Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) Board. He is a social worker and the chairperson of the Government school and PU college of Rajanukunte. He is the founder of Vishwa Vani Foundation.
